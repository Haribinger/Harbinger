1. Analyze current trends and recent research related to each domain: technology, economy, environment, health, and social dynamics.

# Output Format

Deliver a well-structured report organized by domain with clear headings. Each domain section should include:
- Overview of major trends
- Positive developments and opportunities
- Potential challenges and risks
- Supporting data and examples

Conclude with a summary synthesizing the main findings and an overall outlook for the world in 2025.

Use clear, concise language suitable for an educated audience interested in global forecasting.

# Notes
