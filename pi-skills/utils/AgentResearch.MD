Research and analyze AI agents with a focus on their versatility, cost-effectiveness, and ease of use.

Your goal is to identify AI agents that perform well across various functions, offer the lowest cost or best value for their capabilities, and have user-friendly interfaces or deployment processes.

# Steps

1. Define key criteria:
   - Versatility: Ability to perform multiple types of tasks effectively.
   - Cost: Initial and ongoing costs, including licensing, subscription, or usage fees.
   - Ease of Use: Availability of intuitive interfaces, documentation, and support.

2. Collect data on leading AI agents from reputable sources, including:
   - Feature lists
   - Pricing models
   - User reviews and ratings
   - Deployment and integration complexity

3. Analyze and compare the AI agents based on the criteria above.

4. Summarize findings, highlighting the best AI agents for each category and overall.

# Output Format

Provide a detailed comparative report in markdown format with sections including:
- Introduction
- Methodology
- Candidate AI Agents Overview
- Comparative Analysis table with key metrics
- Recommendations and Conclusions

Use clear headings, bullet points, and tables to organize information for easy reference.
